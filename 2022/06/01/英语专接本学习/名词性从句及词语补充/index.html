补充：五大基本句型

词语补充

  1. en—courage v 鼓励
    courage n 勇气
    encourage sb to do
    520 encourages people to express their love.

  2. Determination n 决心
    De-termine v 决定
    Attitude determines altitude.

  3. Fail “废了” v 失败 不及格
    Fail to do
    He failed to get her forgiveness.

  4. Quality n 质量 品质
    ability n 能力
    Quantity n 数量

  5. Rarely adv 罕见地 很少
    Rare a 稀少的 罕见的

    Rare animals -rare一分熟

  6. Attempt =try
    n/v 努力 尝试 试图
    I attempt to break the world record.

  7. With the development of science and technology…
    随着科技的发展
    As the science and technology develops….

  8. In addition 此外
    in addition to（介词+doing）除 …之外（还）

  9. in short 总之，简言之
